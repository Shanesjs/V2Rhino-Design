Your Experience

THE FRONTLINE

Join our expert rangers and pilots on the ground. We provide a hands-on, highly immersive involvement program where you become part of the actual anti-poaching and notching operations.

Aerial Patrol Operations

Climb aboard our specialized pursuit helicopters. Experience low-level tactical flying as our pilots survey the reserves, identify high-risk zones, and monitor the rhino herds from the air.

Rhino Notching Clinics

Work directly alongside world-class veterinarians. Assist in the physical darting process, gathering DNA samples, and inserting highly technical tracking microchips into sedated rhinos.

Ground Tracking Missions

Learn the ancient and critical art of tracking on foot. Join the K9 units and ground patrols to analyze tracks, identify poaching syndicates' movements, and secure the perimeter.

Ready to deploy?

The reserves are active, and our teams are moving. Lock in your dates to join the frontline.
