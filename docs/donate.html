Donate & Make A Difference

WHY DONATE

Donations of any amounts are put to use in a number of ways. You can physically experience your contribution, by joining our teams in rhino notchings and every day projects. Be a voluntary ranger, bring your family, friends, colleagues or customers and join the battle against the cruelty of poaching. Make a donation to actively make a difference. Your contribution goes a long way to protect and save rhino, rangers and field-teams, dedicated to fighting the battle raging against them.

Using an unique technology and methods, we are able to make an impact on the fight against the brutal poaching of rhino and endangered species.

Your experience fee directly empowers our full-time professionals to do their work in protecting the future of our wildlife.

What your donations go towards:

  • Rhino horn trimming – in some reserves all rhino's were dehorned
  • None of the dehorned rhino have been poached as a direct result of these results
  • Surveillance equipment installed
  • Anti-poaching guard equipment
  • Orphaned rhino's have been saved and nurtured back to health
  • Food supplies and medical costs
  • Medical expenses for injured animals
  • DNA sampling that directly leads to the arrest of poachers and more successful conviction
