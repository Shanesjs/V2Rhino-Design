Our Tactical Units

A multi-disciplinary task force operating at the intersection of conservation and combat.

Aerial Patrol

High-altitude surveillance utilizing specialized bush aircraft to monitor vast terrains and detect illegal poaching activity from the skies.

Forefront tech

Utilizing high-end satellite tracking, AI surveillance, and night-vision capabilities to maintain a high-tech shield over our wildlife.

Tactical Vet

Emergency medical stabilization and surgical units capable of operating in extreme field conditions to save survivors from poaching attempts.

Darting Units

Professional darting teams for relocation and medical treatment, ensuring minimal stress and maximum safety for every animal encountered.
