The Frontline Immersion

This is not a safari. This is an invitation to witness and contribute to the high-stakes world of rhino conservation from the absolute front line.

What Makes Us Different?

Rhino Save & Protect offers a tangible way to participate. By booking an experience, you are directly funding a tactical operation that you will observe and support from the field.

  • » Active Anti-Poaching Patrols
  • » Light Aircraft Aerial Surveillance
  • » Professional Vet Treatments
  • » Rhino Relocation and Notching

Operational Integrity

Our success is driven by highly skilled, passionate teams—game pilots, expert trackers, field rangers, and world-class veterinarians working in tandem with the latest infrared and AI surveillance technologies.

By joining us, you are witnessing the bleeding edge of modern conservation efforts. This is a rare access point into a field typically closed to the public.
