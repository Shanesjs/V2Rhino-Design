The Crisis

Over 350 rhinos are poached in the northern reserves in a single year. That's one every day of the year. The crisis is urgent, and it's happening right now.

Rhino Save & Protect is an NGO run by passionate bush-pilots and veterinarians. We donate the majority of the hours required for anti-poaching aerial surveillance and emergency medical treatment.

Tactical Conservation
