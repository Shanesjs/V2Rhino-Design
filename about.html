Our Mission

350+

/ Over 350 rhinos are poached in the northern reserves in 1 year, which equates to 1, every day of the year.

Rhino Save & Protect is an NGO run by passionate bush-pilots and vets and donate most the hours required for the anti-poaching of rhino projects. The field-rangers, darting equipment and medical materials have to be covered but many hours are donated by our team to the cause.

Partners:

  • Rhino 911
  • Madikwe Wildlife Foundation
  • Heli-Game services
  • Mankwe Private Reserve
  • Thabatholo Private Reserve
  • Indigo Helicopters and bush pilots

RHINO SAVE & PROTECT FUND IS DEDICATED TO ENDANGERED SPECIES AND WILDLIFE IN SOUTHERN AFRICA.

Our mission is to save & protect wildlife from poaching – diminishing the number of rhino and endangered species.

What makes us different is that we give you an opportunity to join the rangers & teams to participate in projects on the ground, geared to save our rhino and wild-life.

1 RHINO POACHED EVERY DAY FOR A YEAR!

You can actively get involved and physically experience wildlife conservation projects in a tangible way. The fund makes it possible to deploy unique and modern technologies and methods in the fight against poaching, which is depleting our rhino and wildlife.

The success of these projects, is due to highly skilled and passionate teams, working together, each with extensive experience.

These comprise of specialised game pilots, trackers, rangers and vetenerians, together with the use of forefront technology and methods. These teams work tirelessly to protect our wildlife and are entirely dependent on your help and involvement.
